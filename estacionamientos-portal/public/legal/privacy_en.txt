PRIVACY POLICY - SMARTPARKING SOLUTIONS

Effective date: 05/17/2026

1. Data collected
SmartParking Solutions collects data required for the service, such as email, names, phone number, ID number, vehicle license plate, vehicle model, and parking usage status.

2. Purpose of data use
Data is used exclusively to authenticate users, verify accounts, manage parking lots, display space availability, register movements, and improve the service experience.

3. Location data
The application may request location access to show nearby parking lots and improve user orientation. This information is used only within the app functionality.

4. Information protection
SmartParking applies good practices to protect user information. Access to data is limited to necessary system functions and authorized administrative processes.

5. No data sale
SmartParking Solutions does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Information may only be shared when required for legal or technical service obligations.

6. Emails and notifications
The system may send emails related to verification codes, business request status, approvals, rejections, or important service notices.

7. User rights
The user may request review, update, or deletion of their data through SmartParking Solutions support channels.
